    What is MemeFryer?

    Memes are a big part of modern life, most people in the world have seen a meme at some point. Deep fried memes are a sub-genre of memes. They all involve increasing the gamma, contrast, saturation, & many other image properties.

    Making deep fried memes is not always as easy as it may seem though. You either end up using a free service that leaves a watermark, or end up paying for a software that is easy to get lost in from all the bells & whistles you don't want or need.

    MemeFryer is here to help everyone make deep fried memes with a simple interface, with only the effects you want to use. No fluffy nonsense here!

    The goal is to be able to make memes like these ⬇️⬇️⬇️
